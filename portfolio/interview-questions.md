# Interview Questions

Prepare for these questions based on this project:

1.  Why did you choose Microservices over a Monolith for this project?
2.  How did you handle distributed transactions between the Order and Payment services?
3.  Why did you choose Argo CD (GitOps) over traditional push-based deployments like Jenkins?
4.  How do you secure your Kubernetes cluster?
5.  What is your strategy for monitoring and alerting?
6.  How do you manage secrets in your infrastructure?
7.  If the database goes down, how does your system react?
8.  Explain your Terraform state management strategy.
